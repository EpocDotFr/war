We use Gauges as our website analytics. It is privacy-friendly (read: it doesn't sell or give your informations to third-parties).

Samples

By clicking on the Recognize button on the home page, you understand and agree that some audio will be recorded from your device and then will be sent to our service for analysis.

The audio samples you submit to WAR aren't kept. Once a sample has been analyzed by the audio databases we request, it is deleted. We only store the audio metadata, if the track is found, for statistics purposes.
